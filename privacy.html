Privacy Policy

Effective date:

“Client Publisher” (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a media client tool used by authorized staff to create and publish Instagram Reels via the Instagram Graph API. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • Account information: staff username, basic profile identifiers used for login.
  • Client content & metadata: video URLs, captions, timestamps, and publish status.
  • Tokens & connections: access tokens/IDs needed to connect an Instagram Business or Creator account to our tool.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser info, and logs for security and troubleshooting.

2) How we use information

  • Authenticate staff and authorize actions inside the tool.
  • Call the Instagram Graph API to create media containers, poll processing status, and publish Reels.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service; prevent abuse; comply with legal obligations.

3) Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Contract/Service provision: to provide publishing features requested by your organization/client.
  • Legitimate interests: securing the platform, fraud prevention, and service analytics.
  • Consent: where required for optional features or certain data connections.

4) Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with service providers that help us operate the tool (e.g., hosting, logging, CDN) under confidentiality and security commitments, and as required by law or to protect rights.

5) Instagram data & permissions

We use the Instagram Graph API with minimum scope (including instagram_content_publish) solely to process and publish the content you submit. Tokens are stored securely and used only to provide the service. We do not use Instagram data for advertising profiling or unrelated purposes.

6) Data retention

We keep content, logs, and tokens only as long as needed to operate the service and meet legal/audit requirements. You may request deletion of stored content or account data via the contact email below; we will honor requests subject to legal limits.

7) Security

We apply industry-standard security measures (encryption in transit, restricted access, monitoring). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continually work to protect data from unauthorized access.

8) International transfers

Our providers may process data in various countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

9) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to object or request portability. Contact us to exercise these rights; we will verify your identity and respond as required by law.

10) Children

The service is intended for professional use by authorized staff; it is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
